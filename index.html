Tax Insights & Fast Facts

Stay informed with timely data points and tips that help you plan smarter.

Average Refund

The IRS issued refunds averaging $3,167 for the 2024 filing season. Adjust your withholding early to avoid surprises.

IRS Data Book
Record Retention

Keep tax records for at least three years, but hold on to business asset purchases and real estate documents for seven.

Audit Prep
Startup Deductions

New businesses can deduct up to $5,000 in startup costs the first year. Expenses above that may be amortized over 15 years.

IRC Sec. 195
Mileage Rates

The 2025 standard mileage rate is 67 cents per business mile. Maintain a log to capture every eligible trip.

IRS Notice 2024-87
Child Tax Credit

Families may claim up to $2,000 per qualifying child. Advance payments affect refund totals, so review IRS Letter 6419 when filing.

Family Savings
Estimated Taxes

If you expect to owe $1,000 or more after withholdings, quarterly estimated payments help avoid penalties.
